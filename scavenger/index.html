SCAVENGER: Nomads of Uncharted Worlds is a beautifully illustrated art book and an original campaign setting for D&D 5E, set in a ravaged Bronze Age world where the only hope is an ancient gateway to other mysterious dimensions throughout the multiverse.

Explorers, wanderers, treasure hunters, marauders — all of them are scavengers drawn to this old and alien world. Some seek knowledge, others salvation.

Includes new subclasses, a bestiary, adventure locations, magical items, feats, and a series of core rules changes designed to communicate the vibe of the setting.

Scavenger: Crimson Catalyst is a setting guide, rule expansion, monster manual, and adventure generator for the SCAVENGER setting. First debuted in SCAVENGER: Nomads of Uncharted Worlds, players take on the roles of a group of daredevil scavengers with everything to lose. They must rely on dangerous alien magic in order to survive the end of their world and the insanity of a strange dimension known only as the World Torn Apart. Save history and lives in the Bronze Age Akara, or go through the mysterious Monolith in search of miracles and secrets in the technicolor deathscape that is Torn. This is Scavenger: Crimson Catalyst!

This amazing product contains over 456 pages of content, including:

  • New game modes and produces for expeditions through dying and alien worlds, including Hunts, Countdowns to Cataclysm, and Caravan Mode
  • Over 100 magical items across all tiers of play, including many of which are earned through special Epithets instead of typical Ability Score Improvements
  • Adjustments to core rules to create a more intense and flavorful experience, such as Ability Score Proficiency, Oasis Rests, Visceral Criticals, and more
  • Dozens of stat blocks across all tiers of play and the build-a-monster Impossibility Generator
  • Several ready-to-play campaign structures, three in-depth adventure generators, and dozens of roll tables for customizing your gameplay experience or for grabbing exciting ideas
  • Nearly every page is graced with stunning artwork masterfully created by Dominus specifically for this project.

Also contained within this product are a number of digital gameplay accessories, designed to ease your way into Crimson Catalyst's innovative ruleset.

The SCAVENGER Quickstart is a brief and flavorful introduction to the Scavenger universe. Scavenger is a unique fantasy setting for 5th Edition where the namesake heroes are racing against time to either find a way to prevent their world's imminent extinction, called both the Great Dying and the Cataclysm. The scavengers must traverse an alien dimension of science fantasy, discover the secrets of the Zenith People that once dwelled there, and either find a way to escape their dying homeworld or otherwise stop the Great Dying in its tracks.

The SCAVENGER Quickstart details the world's themes, the unique magic found there, and the cultures of the Bronze Age civilization now facing utter annihilation.
